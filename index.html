    • Why we Started
    ProSine Society

    Students lack scientific exploration because of lack of access to scientific initiatives and quality mentoring, As a result, students from economically weaker backgrounds label science as “not so good fit” and choose other alternatives. As an enthusiast we here are building a social based community where every student interested on basic science and project from model based to production level. And exposure of Additional knowledge on certain exotic resource available around the North-East region of iNDIA which are yet to be known for their use case ..
